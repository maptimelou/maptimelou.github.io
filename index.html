Web Cartography at the University of Kentucky

EPA Violations in Kentucky, 2010-2015

This map depicts the location of 614 violations reported by the Environmental Protection Agency in Kentucky since 2010. The data is from Violation Tracker, which calls itself the “the first national search engine on corporate misconduct.”

245 locations were geocoded with the provided address, 198 locations were derived from the provided coordinates, and 171 locations were approximated by zip code.

  • See the map: EPA Violations in Kentucky, 2011-2015
  • Data Source: Violation Tracker
